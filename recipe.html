Chicken Soup (5 Servings)

First, put a cup of olive oil, one spoon of salt, two carrots, three tomatoes, and four pototoes into the crock-pot. While it's cooking, you could seaon the chicken with pepper and add it to the cooker. Add 6 cups of water and let it cook for 6 hours. 15 minutes before serving, add some pasta to the cooker and cover it until the pasta gets soft. Serve the soup with bread stick. It will last for 4 days in the fridge.

Ingredients

  • c. of olive oil
  • 1 spoon of salt
  • 2 carrots
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 4 pototes
  • 1 chicken
  • 1 cup of pepper
  • 4 bread stick

Directions

  1. Combine the olive oil and salt into a cup
  2. add carrorts, tomatoes and pototoes
  3. Season the chicken with pepper and salt
  4. Drop the ingredients into the cooker
  5. Drop the chicken and let it cook for 6 hours
  6. Serve with Pasta and bread stick
